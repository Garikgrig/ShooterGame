This is a shooting game called Shooter Game.You are a soldier and you must
fight with all robots and reach to the blue cube,which is the finish. Use A,W,S,D
keys to control your character, press W+Shift to run,use Mouse to rotate,
Mouse Left Button to shoot,Space to jump and ESC
to exit game.If you want to pause game press Z.Find door keys and Press E to open doors.
Use Mouse Scroll to change guns. There are 3 types of guns in the game.
Common(blue),Rare(orange) and Epic(purple).Kill all enmies and go to finish
to win the game. You can find this description in the "How to play" in the game.
